Crowdog is a musician and songwriter from the Eastern Shore who performs as a solo act. Playing a range of music such as rock, blues, indie and pop, He offers a show that has a variety of songs for all types of venues. He is active in the music scene of Onancock Virginia as a singer and guitarist for The Southern Fog Band. Crowdog is currently booking for the 2026 season.
